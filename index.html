New Life

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and teach kids and teenagers about the importance of recycling and living sustainably. We want to make recycling a part of everyday life by encouraging eco-friendly habits. By working with schools, we aim to build a network of kids who understand their impact on the environment. Our projects help reduce waste and keep it out of landfills through creative recycling solutions. We believe that by giving children the knowledge and tools to make sustainable choices, we can create a greener future for our planet.

What we do

Exciting workshops are available for all ages, featuring hands-on activities designed to spark imagination. Participants can choose projects that, depending on their age and skill level, ensure a fun and appropriately challenging experience. The workshops use locally sourced materials, promoting sustainability and community involvement, creating a circular economy. Collaboration with local schools will allow a better learning experience.
